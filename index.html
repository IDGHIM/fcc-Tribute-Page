Sheffield

- The competition of all champions -

  • History of Sheffield

    • Sheffield is the biggest powerlifting event that takes place every year. To participate in the World Championships in Sheffield, you must achieve a total of more than 95% of the world record.

    Sheffield aims to elect the champion of all categories for women and men. To determine who is the champion of champions, each successful bar gets a percentage and the highest percentage wins.

    This competition offers spectable because the best athletes in the discipline participate, and especially that world records can fall at any time.

    The prize is a cash prize, a trophy and the title of champion for the current year.
